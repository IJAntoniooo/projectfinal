Brief Information:

Rajon Rondo joined the NBA in the year 2006. He went to the draft and he was selected with the 21st pick of the Phoenix Suns which was traded to the Boston Celtics almost immediately. He started his early years with the Boston Celtics until the year 2014. He was already the captain of the team when the team decided to trade him for players and picks. Before leaving the Beantown, he won the prestigious NBA Championship trophy in the year 2008 with Paul Pierce, Kevin Garnett, Ray Allen, and others. During his one year stint with Dallas Mavericks, the team made it to the NBA playoffs. During his tenure with the Chicago Bulls, he made it to the playoffs alongside Jimmy Butler and Dwyane Wade but the team was defeated in six games by the Boston Celtics. In the previous NBA season, he and Anthony Davis led the New Orleans Pelicans to a second-round exit against the Golden State Warriors.
